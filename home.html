ABOUT

Mharnie's Kitchenette was born from the heart and vision of its founder, combining the name of the owner with a passion for creating memorable flavors. Our goal is simple: to share our love for food by letting the world experience the unique dishes we've perfected. Each recipe is a reflection of the care and dedication we put into our culinary creations, inspired by tradition, but made with our personal touch. At Mharnie's Kitchenette, we believe food connects people, and we're excited to share the flavors we're most proud of with you. Come, taste the difference that comes from cooking with passion!

SPECIALTY

STEAK

Savor the rich, juicy flavors of our perfectly cooked steak! Each cut is carefully selected and grilled to perfection, bringing out its natural tenderness and taste. Whether you prefer your steak rare, medium, or well-done, we ensure every bite is full of flavor and satisfaction. Treat yourself to the ultimate steak experience!

SISIG

Get ready to indulge in the sizzling goodness of our Sisig! A Filipino classic, our Sisig is made from tender, crispy pork, marinated and seasoned to perfection, served on a hot plate with a burst of flavors in every bite. Whether you like it spicy or mild, this dish is a perfect blend of savory, tangy, and crunchy, making it an irresistible treat for any meal!
